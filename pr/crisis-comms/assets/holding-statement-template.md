# Holding statement templates (fillable)

Fill the brackets with verified facts only. Leave any unconfirmed value as a bracketed placeholder rather than guessing. Keep to 3–5 sentences.

---

## Generic holding statement

> We are aware of [SITUATION] affecting [AFFECTED GROUP], and we understand how concerning this is. Our team is [ACTION UNDERWAY], and [PRIORITY — e.g., the safety of those affected / protecting your data] is our top priority. We will share verified information as soon as we have it, and will provide an update by [SPECIFIC TIME/DATE]. For the latest, please visit [OWNED CHANNEL].

## Data / security incident

> We recently became aware of [a security incident involving / unauthorized access to] [SYSTEM]. We immediately [CONTAINMENT ACTION] and engaged [forensics/experts]. We are working to understand exactly what happened and who is affected, and we are committed to keeping [affected group] informed. We will provide a further update by [TIME]. If you are affected, [INTERIM ACTION / where to get help].

## Safety / product issue

> We have identified [ISSUE] with [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. The safety of [affected group] comes first, so we are [IMMEDIATE SAFETY ACTION — e.g., advising customers to stop using / halting distribution]. If you have [PRODUCT], please [SPECIFIC SAFETY INSTRUCTION]. We are investigating the cause and will share more by [TIME]. For help, contact [CHANNEL].

## Allegation / reputational story

> We take [the concerns raised / these allegations] seriously and are [reviewing the matter carefully / cooperating fully]. We are committed to [RELEVANT VALUE], and we will act on what we find. We will share further information once the facts are established, and expect to update by [TIME].

---

### Checklist before issuing
- [ ] Every fact is verified (or left as a bracket).
- [ ] People affected are addressed before company impact.
- [ ] No speculation on cause, blame, or numbers.
- [ ] A specific next-update time is given (and will be met).
- [ ] Legal has reviewed if any liability/regulatory exposure exists.
- [ ] It points to a single owned channel for updates.
